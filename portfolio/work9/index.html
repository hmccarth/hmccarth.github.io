Doodles Revisited: Getting to Know the Wacom Intuos Graphics Tablet

This is a fun side project I’ve recently started working on as a way to learn how to use a Wacom graphics tablet. A few weeks ago I came across some of my childhood sketchbooks and had the idea to recreate them in Adobe Illustrator. I am a new owner of a graphics tablet and have been enjoying my time learning the tablet’s various capabilities.

–

I hope to continue this project in the coming months when I find free time between finishing up my undergraduate degree and applying for jobs. For the time being, I hope you enjoy these quite wacky childhood drawings.
