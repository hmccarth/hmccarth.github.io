Hi there! My name is Henry McCarthy and I’m a senior at Macalester College majoring in Geography and minoring in Data Science and Environmental Studies. I’m originally from San Diego, California but for the past few years I’ve been based out of St. Paul, Minnesota while I complete my undergraduate degree. I’ve thoroughly enjoyed exploring the Twin Cities during my time at Macalester, and to the surprise of friends and family in California, I’ve also enjoyed the long Minnesota winters.

My academic interests seem to change with just about every class I take, but for the past few years I’ve honed in on the environmental side of geography and have worked to develop my technical skills in GIS and data science. While my academic and professional endeavors have taken me to one of the most rural countries in the world, Mongolia, I also remain passionate about environmental issues in urban centers. I am excited about urban ecology and sustainable city planning and I’m eager to explore new cities in the United States and beyond.

Cartography and design are two of my greatest passions and I hope to find a career path that allows me to blend my interest and experience in data and environmental sciences with my attention to aesthetics and visualizations. I have been fortunate enough to have academic and professional experiences that have allowed me to practice my cartography and design skills while also gaining wonderful experience in the sciences.
